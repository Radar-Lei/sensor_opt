# Quick Summary: Strengthen PeMS7_228 Low-Budget Enhanced Search

Completed: 2026-05-27T06:09:20+08:00

## Result

The enhanced-search route for PeMS7_228 at 10% budget is not strong enough to promote into the TR-B manuscript.

The active-set enhanced TRACE-BiOpt-only diagnostic over seeds 25-34:

- improves TRACE-BiOpt mean MAE from 3.5394 to 3.5145;
- improves the mean seed-matched margin against the best pre-registered non-BiOpt baseline from -0.0162 to -0.0411;
- wins 7/10 seeds;
- gives paired t-test `p=0.0671` and Wilcoxon `p=0.0527`.

Because seed-level dominance is still mixed, this does not satisfy the standard used for the PeMS7_1026 30% enhanced-search paper diagnostic.

## Full-Exchange Check

To test whether the remaining failures were caused by active-set truncation, complete one-exchange search was run on failed seeds 25, 29, and 31. It improved active-set MAE slightly but still lost to the seed-matched best baseline on all three:

| Seed | Best baseline MAE | Active enhanced MAE | Full-exchange MAE | Full delta |
|------|-------------------|---------------------|-------------------|------------|
| 25 | 3.3982 | 3.4743 | 3.4259 | 0.0277 |
| 29 | 3.4508 | 3.4839 | 3.4722 | 0.0214 |
| 31 | 3.8926 | 3.9675 | 3.9606 | 0.0680 |

## Decision

Do not add a PeMS7_228 10% enhanced-search table or stronger manuscript claim. The current main-table wording remains correct: TRACE-BiOpt has lower mean MAE in the Stage15 main table, but this row is not paired-significant.
