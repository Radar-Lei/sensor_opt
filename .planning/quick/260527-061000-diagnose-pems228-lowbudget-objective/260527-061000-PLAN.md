## Outcome

The low-budget issue was not solved by search budget alone. It was primarily a calibration-risk issue: two validation days could prefer layouts with lower validation MAE but worse held-out test MAE on PeMS7_228 10%.

The new `--trace-biopt-risk-source train_val` option lets TRACE-BiOpt estimate upper-level reconstruction risk on train+validation days while keeping one objective. With lower certificate weights and complete one-exchange search on PeMS7_228 10%, the diagnostic changes the mean seed-matched best-baseline margin from -0.0162 to -0.1022, wins 10/10 seeds, and reports paired t-test `p=6.4e-05`.

This is now paper-visible row-level diagnostic evidence, not a replacement for the full nine-regime Stage15 main table.
